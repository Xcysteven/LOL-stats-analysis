Is Summoner's Rift Fair?

A Game Balance Audit of League of Legends (2025 Season)

By: Steven Xu

Introduction

This project investigates the inherent design biases in the 2025 League of Legends map. Specifically, we audit whether the "Blue Side" has a geometric advantage in securing neutral objectives like the new Atakhan, and if this creates an unfair win probability.

Hypothesis & Cleaning

Null Hypothesis: The map is balanced. Probability of securing objectives is equal for both sides.

Alternative Hypothesis: The map is biased. One side secures objectives significantly more often.
